About

From MSP support to cloud-native delivery.

I'm a Cloud Support / IT Operations Engineer based in Houston. My career started in graphic design at Venti Air Products, where I picked up the IT and operations work nobody else wanted — managing inventory systems, handling vendor coordination, and keeping the office running.

That detour turned into a deliberate pivot. I went through MyComputerCareer's IT program, stacked CompTIA A+, Network+, Security+, CIOS, CSIS, and Linux Essentials, and joined IronEdge Group, one of Houston's largest MSPs. Over 15 months I went from Onboarding Technician to IT Support Technician — running 100+ employee AD/M365 administration, Tier-2 escalations, and 50+ tickets a week with a 95% first-contact resolution rate.

In late 2025 I joined CIRculate, a stealth startup, as the founding technical hire. I shipped a full-stack web app (Next.js + React on Vercel, Neon Postgres, Auth0) in 60 days, ran the production environment end-to-end, and handled tooling decisions across the SaaS stack — Stripe, Resend, and others. Real ops work in a startup setting.

Now I'm looking for a Cloud Support or IT Operations role where my MSP fundamentals and cloud-deployment experience can both pay off. Houston metro preferred — open to onsite or hybrid.
